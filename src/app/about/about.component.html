About the Author

Hi! Welcome to Heesuk's Kitchen! My name is Heesuk Jang. I am the author and cook of Heesuk's Kitchen. I am also a mom of two beautiful girls ages 7 and 9.

At the end of 2017, I started developing Heesuk's Kitchen to share some of my favorite Korean dishes that my family loves. Both my girls grew up largely appreciating Korean family tradition and cultural heritage through food. My family loves to eat healthy while enjoying flavorful and appealing foods and cooking. Food in my family means more than just feeding nutritions. It means love and soul.

Here I share how to cook and enjoy the Korean foods that we love.

My Hometown

I am from Chuncheon, which is the capital city of Gangwon Province, South Korea. It is located about 47 miles (74.8 km) east-north of Seoul. Chuncheon is one of the famous tourist cities especially as the city of lakefront, surrounded by three famous lakes and Sinyeoungang river. The popular riverside tourist destinations has many places to visit, including Nami Island, Jundo Island, Chungpyung Temple, Soyanggang River, Samaksan mountain, Gugok Waterfall, Jepdarigol Natural Forest and more.

Top Tour Sights in Chuncheon
Chungpyung Temple 청평사

The Cheongpyeongsa tourist area is located 19km away from downtown Chuncheon.

Nami Island 남이섬

Nami Island possesses beautiful nature and scenery all year round. It is a place of shareing love.

Jade Garden 제이드정원

Jade Garden was opened in April 2011 with the motto of a small Europe in the forest.

Gugok Fall 구곡폭포

This is a 50m tall waterfall located at the foot of Bonghwasan (봉화산) Mt. in Chuncheon.

Food Delights in Chuncheon
Dakgalbi 닭갈비

Dakgalbi (spicy stir-friend chicken) is the most famous food of Chuncheon.

Makkusu 막국수

Buckwheat is used to make the noodles and it is combined with kimchi, added with vegetables.

Memiljeonbyung 메밀전병

Memil-jeonbyung or buckwheat pancake is a variety of buchimgae, or Korean pancake.

Gamjatang 감자탕

Gamja-tang or pork back-bone stew is a spicy soup made from the spine or neck bones of a pig.
